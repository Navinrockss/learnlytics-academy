Lesson 1 — Introduction to Excel

Excel is a spreadsheet tool used to work with numbers, data, and analysis. It is widely used in all industries including business, finance, HR, sales, marketing, data analysis, and more.

Excel Interface

  • Workbook — Excel file that holds sheets

  • Cells — Intersection of a row & column
  • Ribbon — Toolbar with buttons
